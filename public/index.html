Good Toy Matters

An excellent toy should be open-ended, and improve the child's creativity.

The lack of children's creativity is a huge social concern for most parents and teachers.Many parents do not know the importance of children's creativity and do not pay attention to the harm brought by electronic products.


For a child, a handcrafted kit is a great toy; it can not only improve the child's hands on experiences, but also enhance their creative skills and sensory experiences. This game allows children to communicate better and interact with others, so they produce enthusiasm towards creativity.
