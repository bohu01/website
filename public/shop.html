Handcrafty Story Stage Kit

An excellent toy should be open-ended, and improve the child's creativity.

$99

The kit contains a small stage, a Story Card and a wide range of materials and tools to build characters and backgrounds. Children can easily build their small stage according to their own designs without the help of parents. After children have created their own stories with Story Cards, they can use the material from the kit to create their own characters. The kit also provides props for the characters, including sticks and cords that help them stand up. This way, the children can attach them to the back of the characters, allowing them to control their characters’ during performances.
