About Handcrafty

My product is built around having children create their characters and showcasing them to others in order to enhance their creativity and imagination.

Three years ago, I found an article on “Children's Creativity” in the local newspaper. It mentioned how most children’s creativity has been in a steady decline over the past 50 years. After reading this article, I developed a strong interest in this topic. I like children! I enjoy dealing with them and this sparked an interest in all articles about their education. As a graphic designer, I would like to use the visual language that I work with, to benefit the spark children need to foster creativity. Utilizing visual language to help children is the topic I have chosen for my thesis project.


This game allows children to communicate better and interact with others, so they produce enthusiasm towards creativity. There is a lack of exchange and communication between children and their peers. It seems, educators don’t know how to improve children's creativity, and this card game can hopefully solve this problem. Parents and children can play with this card game while encouraging their children to think out of the box and be creative.


In this website, users can purchase our products online, and find out background information of the brand and corporate culture. Through this website, users can read and understand the importance of creativity for children, and how to properly improve their children's creative thinking skills.
