Why Handcrafty

Provide craft materials and creative themes for children

Handcrafted kit is a great toy. it can not only improve the child's hands on experiences, but also enhance their creative skills and sensory experiences.

The biggest difference between our brand and other similar competing companies is that not only do I want to improve children’s hands-on experiments through a variety of materials incorporating manual work, but I also aim to incorporate new ideas, which increase mental stimulants by creating their own stories and characters.

Our product advocates communication. Many parents want to improve their children's learning abilities, but are not sure how to achieve this. This is where the idea for the stage come from. We came up with building a small stage for them to showcase their characters and stories to their parents, teachers, and friends. This way, they can gain more confidence and provide better exchange and communication with parents and friends. We also developed a card game that makes this product more interesting and participatory.

The Design

BUILD YOU OWN KIT FOR YOUR CHILDREN

Visual Design is an important part of my work, including which style is acceptable and liked most by children. To find a better visual style for our product, we found relevant pictures and themes as references. After asking parents and the children for their opinions, I wound up choosing hand-drawn line drawing styles and carefully drew out every pattern for the whole kit.

The small stage design was a great challenge. To build it, we drew countless sketches to analyze the structure of the stage. Every angle and the thickness of the board have been carefully measured and calculated, so it allows children to create a small stage correctly with minimum direction.

Handcrafted’s typeface was created and hand-drawn by ourselves. To keep our brand image more consistent with children's preferences, I have designed this very cute style font and applied it to the kit design. The font style is unified with my overall hand-drawn style brand.
